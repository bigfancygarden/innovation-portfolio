## Description

*To be developed.*

The capstone microcredential of the certificate program. Focuses on building and sustaining the client and community relationships that make a creative practice viable over time — synthesizing the cultural literacy of Stack 1, the institutional awareness of Stack 2, and the practice formation tools of Stack 3 into a coherent professional identity.

Completion of MC9, combined with MC1–MC8, earns the certificate designation.

The microcredential is built around a portfolio of case examples that can be selected and localized by instructor and student. Students are expected to bring their own emerging client or community relationship as a self-directed case.
