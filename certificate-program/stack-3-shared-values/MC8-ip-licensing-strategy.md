## Description

*To be developed.*

Equips students with the conceptual and practical tools to navigate intellectual property and licensing strategy in creative industries contexts — with particular attention to the tensions between cultural producers' ownership of their work and institutional interests in distributing, monetizing, or archiving it.

The microcredential is built around a portfolio of case examples that can be selected and localized by instructor and student depending on context.
